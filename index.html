Our Guarantees

Sonic Fast Shipping

We know how frustrating it is to wait for your products to be delivered. That's why retro Revolution has partnered with Epic Shipping so you can take advantage of their Ovanite™ shipping system. Make an order by 3pm and we guarantee that your order will arrive the following day! (New Zealand based customers only.)

Quality Guaranteed

We only source our products from highly reputable suppliers. We also thoroughly inspect and test every single product we receive to futher ensure our products are of the highest quality. Our technicians are fully certified by the Retro Technician's Association of New Zealand.

30 Days Return Policy

In the unlikely case where you are not completely satisfied with our products, we will happily provide you with a full refund, no questions asked. Simply send us a a message and return the items within 30 days.
