About Us

Since our debut in 2018, Retro Revolution has provided New Zealand and the world with top quality retro products. With a massive range to choose from, we solidify ourselves as New Zealand's premier retro game store.

Expansion

In August of 2019, we decided to expand our business internationally to cater to a growing demand for high quality retro products. To achieve this, we partnered with Epic Shipping, a splendid logistic company that makes sure our products remains high quality during the delivery phase, all the way until it reaches you. Of course, this partnership also benefited our original Auckland and New Zealand customers and supporters. New Zealand customers are able to take advantage their Ovanite™ Shipping system, which is guaranteed to deliver your order in only a day!

The Future

In the near future, Retro Revolution is planning to open up additional stores and warehouses in not only other parts of New Zealand, but world wide! This move is to ensure all of our customers will be able to enjoy the retro gaming culture to the fullest extent. Additional physical stores wil allow us to host our famous retro competitions across the world and drastically decrease shipping time for international customers, as well as allow for the possibility of same day shipping for our domestic customers.
