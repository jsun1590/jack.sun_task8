Are You Up To The Challenge?

On the 21st day of every month, we host a massive retro gaming tournament at our Auckland store. These tournaments are a perfect opportunity for retro gaming enthusiasts such as yourself to meet other like minded people and battle each other.

Rules

Participants must:

  • behave in a respectful manner toward tournament officials, other tournament participants, and spectators and refrain from unsporting conduct at all times.

  • Pay a $10 entry fee upon registration.

  • Register at least 30 minutes before the main event at the front desk on the day of the event or pre-register online.

  • Have fun! :)

Prizes

Of course, a tournament is nothing without any prizes! Check out our epic prizes down below:

  • 1st Prize: 1 free game from our store, valued at $70 or less; PLUS a $50 store voucher.

  • 2nd Prize: 1 free game from our store, valued at $50 or less.

  • 3rd Prize: 1 free game from our store, valued at $30 or less.

  • Participation: All participants will receive a free custom made T-Shirt!
